# Génération de la mascotte

## Prompt bloquant
2D flat vector animation of Mogogo, a friendly blue owl wizard with a blue wizard hat and big black glasses. 
Green screen background (chroma key). High contrast, NO shadows, NO gradients, NO lighting effects. Flat colors only.
Style: Clean lines, solid colors, consistent character design. The green color is strictly reserved for the background. Looping animation.
Action: 

# Questioning Phase

    The owl listens to the user's answer and writes on its parchment.

    The owl ponders and writes on its parchment.

    The owl ponders, takes out a grimoire, and searches through it for information.

    The owl scratches its head and writes on its parchment.

# Pivot Phase

    The owl shakes its head "no" and crosses something out on its parchment.

# Solution Phase

    The owl looks at the user and jumps for joy, happy to have found an activity for them.

    The owl does a happy dance, full of joy.

    The owl pulls out a magic wand and makes sparks appear.
